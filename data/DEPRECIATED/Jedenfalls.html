in any case adv

Ich werde jedenfalls anrufen, sobald ich mehr wei. — In any case, I will call as soon as I know more.

at least adv

at any rate adv

Diesmal habe ich meine Lektion jedenfalls gelernt. — This time, at any rate, I have learned my lesson.

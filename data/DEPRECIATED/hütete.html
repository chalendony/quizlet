“er/sie/es hütete” could be 3rd person simple past

hüten (jdn./etw.Akk ~) verb—

guard sb./sth. v (guarded, guarded)

look after sb./sth. v

tend v (tended, tended)

Der Schäfer hütet seine Schafe auf der Wiese. — The shepherd tends his sheep on the meadow.

shepherd v

Er hütet seine Schafe mithilfe eines Hundes. — He shepherds his sheep with the help of a dog.

mind sb./sth. v

Mein Ehemann hütet die Kinder, während ich auf der Arbeit bin. — My husband minds the children when I am at work.

herd v

Er erzog den Schäferhund dazu, seine Schafe zu hüten. — He trained the shepherd dog to herd his sheep.
less common:
keep watch over v (cattle)

Examples:

Er hütete sich wohl, davon zu sprechen.—

He was careful, though, not to speak about it.

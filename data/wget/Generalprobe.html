Generalprobe noun, feminine—

dress rehearsal n (often used)

Die Generalprobe ist die letzte Probe vor der Auffhrung. — The dress rehearsal is the last rehearsal before the performance.
less common:
general rehearsal n
·
final rehearsal n
·
last rehearsal n

Generalproben noun, plural—

dress rehearsals pl
